    Hokkaido University


    Hokkaido University is one of the oldest, largest, and most prestigious universities in Japan.
    Boasting the largest number of faculties of Japan’s national universities, we cover almost
    all areas of the humanities and social and natural sciences and are leader in research activities
    in Japan and the world. Hokkaido university`s alumni include a Nobel laureate, business leaders,
    research pioneers, artists and writers.
